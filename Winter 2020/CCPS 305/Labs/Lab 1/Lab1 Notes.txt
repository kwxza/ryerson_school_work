Stack (LIFO):
    The stack class will have a pointer to the 
    last element added.
    It will also have a length tracker that is
    incremented in push() and decremented in pop().
    - push()
    - pop()
    - length()

Queue (FIFO):
    The queue class will have pointers to the 
    head and tail elements.
    The length tracker variable will be incremented
    in enqueue() and decremented in dequeue().
    - enqueue()
    - dequeue()
    - length()
